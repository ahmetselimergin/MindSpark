# Privacy Policy — Mind Spark

_Effective date: 23 July 2026_

Mind Spark ("the app", "we") is a mobile puzzle game. This policy explains what
data the app collects and how it is used. Play does not require an account.

## Data we collect

Your game progress (levels, stars, lives) is stored **locally on your device**.

To display ads and understand app usage, the app uses third‑party SDKs that may
collect:

- Device and technical information (device model, OS version, language, network type)
- Advertising identifier (e.g. Google Advertising ID) and app‑instance identifiers
- IP address and approximate (coarse) location derived from it
- Ad interaction and app usage events (impressions, clicks, sessions)

## Third‑party services

- **Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK)** — serves banner and interstitial
  ads. Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy — how Google uses
  data from apps that use its services:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Google processes data as described in its own policies.

## How we use data

- To display and measure advertising
- To analyze app performance and usage
- To maintain and improve the app

## Children

Mind Spark is intended for a **general audience aged 13 and over**. It is not
directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal
information from children under 13.

## Data retention

Local game progress remains on your device until you delete the app or clear its
data. Data collected by third‑party SDKs is retained per their policies.

## Your choices

- Reset or limit ad personalization via your device settings
  (Android: Settings → Google → Ads).
- Clear local game data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling.

## Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date above reflects the latest version.
